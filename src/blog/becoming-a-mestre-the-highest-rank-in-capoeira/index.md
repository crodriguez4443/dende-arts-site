        <td>## Grão mestre - the Mestre of Mestres

In actuality, “Mestre” is not really the last chord in some systems of Capoeira. <br>Some chord systems have levels to their Mestre chords until they reach the last one in line. <br>In most of these systems, everyone has the same label of “Mestre” but their chord offer a hierarchical structure. <br>In some cases, capoeira groups will use the term Grão Mestre to represent the last stage in the hierarchy of Mestres. <br>In other cases Grão Mestre is the leader of the group. <br>And in other systems there is no "official" Grão Mestre chord. <br>Everyone's journey to reach the title is different, but one of the prerequisites is a lot of responsibility to the Capoeira community.</td>
